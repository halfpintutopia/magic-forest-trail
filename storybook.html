Bartli's Story

Bartli lives in Braunwald. He know all the mountains, forests and streams.

He is the guardian of a precious stone. The crystal helps sick or injured animals. It is Barli's duty to look after the animals in the moutain.

The job was appointed to him be the Dwarf King, who resides on the top of a rocky plateau in his castle.

Bartli is happy everyday, he finds joy in the the beauty of the area. He often hikes to Seblensee, lots of people gather here. Luckily Bartli is invisible to humans.

At the end of the summer there is festival for the full moon. All the dwarves gather together at the castle. There is music and lots of dancing.

Before the festival, at the end of summer, Bartli prepares for winter. He collects leaves for his bed.

After the celebrations, he goes home quickly, before the moon disappears behing the mountain, to get cosy and warm in he bed of leaves. He hibernates, sleeping all through autumn and winter.

Occasionally, dwarfs enjoy the celebration a little too much, and stay up very late. If a dwarf is not back to their dwelling before the moon goes behind the mountain, their full beard falls off.

If this ever happens, the dwarfs work for the Dwarf King in the mines or up the mountain. They hammer at the rocks looking for precious stones.
