About Bartli the Dwarf

Bartli is a dwarf who lives in Braunwald. He was born in the mountains so he knows everything about forests and its trees and herbs as well as the beautiful animals that live there. Bartli the Dwarf is the guardian of a precious stone - the Dwarfen Crystal. The crystal helps sick or injured animals. It is Barli's duty to look after the animals in the mountains. The job was appointed to him by the Dwarf King, who resides in his castle on the top of a rocky plateau.

News / Upcoming Events

Every Summer holiday Bartli the Dwarf needs the children's help to find the secret ingredients for his gemstone polish. Every Saturday during July and August guided tours including treasure hunts are available for booking. Plan at least half a day for this fun family event. The treasure hunt starts and ends at Grotzenbüel. Unguided tours are also available all July and August. Reservations for the guided tours are recommended.
